App Development

- Mobile App Development (Stockholm University)

In this course, we got into teams of 5-6 people to come up with an innovative game idea, to be built in Unity 3D and to be published on Google Play Store. We spent a total of two months of work developing a top-down shooter game for a mobile interface (Android) which was later published.

My role:
My tasks took place in the creative field; using Adobe Photoshop for creating mood boards, a feature graphic as well as a few sprites for our game. I was also working with audio and visuals, putting together a 40-second long game trailer in Adobe Premiere Pro.

What I learned from it:
- Getting a perspective of the game industry with all the pieces that have to work together when developing a game.
- Setting up an environment and creating character animations in cross-platform game engine Unity 3D.
- Creating selling content for the game through text and video.
