Social Media Marketing Plan

- Social Media Marketing (University of Oklahoma)

This marketing course focused on the tactics of the most common global social media platforms, mainly Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. During the course, we were assigned to a group project which was based on helping a local bookstore to attract more customers, mainly by focusing on increasing their social media presence. To help them, we analyzed their social media platforms and developed a marketing plan including goals and strategies, target group analysis, tactics for the company’s three platforms, sample content and an editorial calendar.

My role:
I got to dig deeper into Facebook as a platform as I analyzed the company’s presence, post frequency as well as their followers’ activity on the page. I also developed general goals and strategies as well as sample content based on their needs and general social media recommendations.

What I learned from it:
- Putting together a social media marketing plan.
- Analyzing a target group to develop primary and secondary example personas.
- Creating a content calendar that is cohesive with an existing brand’s content.
- Different types of marketing tactics, such as inclusive marketing.
